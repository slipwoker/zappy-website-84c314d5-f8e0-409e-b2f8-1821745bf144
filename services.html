Home Network Stabilization

A remote professional experienced frequent network dropouts, slow speeds, and connectivity issues that disrupted video conferences and file transfers. The home office setup lacked proper security measures, leaving personal and work data vulnerable.

Our team performed a complete network audit, upgraded router firmware, optimized channel settings, and implemented network segmentation to separate work and personal devices. We also installed endpoint protection and configured automated backups. The result was a stable, secure home office environment with 99.9% uptime.

Before

Risk Level: Medium – Unsecured network

Stability: Frequent disconnections, slow performance

Recovery Readiness: No backup system in place

After
