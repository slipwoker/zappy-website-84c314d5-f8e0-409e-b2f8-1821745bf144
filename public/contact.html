Common Questions

We understand that IT security consulting and cybersecurity can raise many questions. Here are answers to the most frequently asked questions about our services, processes, and what you can expect when working with us.

What information do you need to start working with us?

We'll need an overview of your current IT infrastructure, including hardware, software, network topology, and any existing security measures. We also request information about your business operations, compliance requirements, and any specific security concerns you may have. Don't worry if you don't have everything documented—we can help you compile this information during our initial assessment.

How do you handle privacy and access to our systems?

We take data privacy extremely seriously. All our consultants sign comprehensive NDAs, and we follow strict access control protocols. We only request the minimum access necessary to perform our work, and all access is logged and monitored. We comply with industry standards including ISO 27001 and GDPR requirements, ensuring your sensitive information remains protected at all times.

Can you help us after a suspicious login or security incident?

Absolutely. We offer rapid incident response services for cyber security concerns. If you detect suspicious activity, unauthorized access attempts, or potential breaches, contact us immediately. Our team can quickly assess the situation, contain the threat, investigate the incident, and implement remediation measures. We also provide post-incident analysis and recommendations to prevent future occurrences.

Do you offer ongoing support after the initial implementation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive ongoing support and maintenance packages. Cybersecurity is not a one-time project—it requires continuous monitoring, updates, and adaptation to new threats. Our support services include regular security assessments, patch management, threat monitoring, employee training updates, and 24/7 emergency response. We can tailor support packages to match your specific needs and budget.

What types of devices and systems do you support?

We work with a wide range of IT infrastructure including Windows, Linux, and macOS systems, mobile devices (iOS and Android), network equipment from major vendors, cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), and various enterprise applications. Our expertise covers both on-premises and cloud-based environments, as well as hybrid configurations. If you have specialized or legacy systems, we can assess compatibility during our initial consultation.

What should we do before starting the engagement?

Before we begin, we recommend creating complete backups of all critical systems and data. Document your current passwords and access credentials in a secure location. Compile a list of all software licenses and vendor contacts. Inform your team about the upcoming security assessment so they're prepared to cooperate. Finally, identify a primary point of contact from your organization who can facilitate communication and provide necessary approvals throughout the project.

How do you stay current with evolving cybersecurity threats?

Our team maintains active certifications from leading security organizations and participates in continuous professional development. We monitor threat intelligence feeds, security bulletins, and industry research daily. We're members of information sharing communities and regularly attend cybersecurity conferences. This commitment to ongoing education ensures we can protect your organization against the latest threats and implement cutting-edge security solutions.
